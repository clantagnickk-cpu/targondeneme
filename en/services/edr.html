ENDPOINT SECURITY – ADVANCED THREAT DETECTION AND RESPONSE

In corporate network architectures, endpoints such as client computers, laptops, mobile devices, and servers are among the most frequently targeted components for cyber attacks. Therefore, endpoint security forms the foundation of modern information security strategies.

Under the provided service, Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) solutions are integrated into endpoints, and all behaviors occurring on the devices are monitored in real time through these systems. Malware activities, unusual process executions, unauthorized file accesses, and user movements are evaluated by advanced analytic engines.

Alongside the deployed security solutions, antivirus, anti-malware, behavioral analysis, sandbox integration, threat intelligence feeds, and automated response mechanisms are activated. Real-time alerts are delivered to system administrators, and immediate actions such as isolation, quarantine, and process termination can be executed during suspicious events.

Moreover, security measures such as software permissions, USB device access, network connection permissions, and screen lock behaviors for endpoints can be defined centrally through policy management.

Endpoint security service does not merely provide protection against malware; it also strengthens post-incident analysis processes and ensures visibility and control across corporate systems. In this way, all endpoint devices of the enterprise are proactively protected and managed.
