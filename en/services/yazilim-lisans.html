SOFTWARE LICENSE MANAGEMENT

The license management of software used in corporate IT infrastructures is of critical importance for both cost control and legal compliance. Under this service, the creation of your business's software inventory and the tracking and management of license statuses are provided.

During the software license management process, all software used within the organization is identified, license statuses are checked, and necessary updates are planned. License durations, usage rights, and renewal dates are tracked in a centralized system.

Solutions offered under this service:

  • Creating and updating software inventory
  • License status tracking and reporting
  • License renewal planning
  • Software usage analysis
  • Cost optimization
  • Legal compliance audit

Thanks to this service, your business can effectively manage software licenses, avoid unnecessary costs, and minimize legal compliance risks.
