VULNERABILITY SCANNING – DETECTION OF SECURITY FLAWS AND RISK ASSESSMENT

To maintain the security of corporate systems sustainably and to take proactive measures against external threats, vulnerability scanning is a critical step. This service aims to systematically detect security flaws in the IT infrastructure and report potential risks by prioritizing them.

Within the scope of the vulnerability scanning service; detailed analyses are performed on servers, client devices, network devices, web applications, and services, identifying known security flaws (CVEs), misconfigurations, open ports, weak passwords, and outdated software.

Scans are conducted based on internationally recognized security databases (NVD, CVSS, OWASP, etc.), and each vulnerability is classified in terms of impact level, ease of exploitation, and potential damage. Furthermore, corrective measures and system improvement recommendations for critical vulnerabilities are presented along with their technical details.

Vulnerability scanning processes are repeated at regular intervals to prevent new risks that may arise in systems over time. All relevant outputs are presented in the form of executive-level readable summary reports and detailed technical analysis documents for technical teams.

This service contributes to helping organizations comply with information security policies, meet regulatory and audit requirements, and structurally increase their cybersecurity awareness.
