DATA BACKUP – SECURE DATA BACKUP AND BUSINESS CONTINUITY SOLUTIONS

Data backup processes are one of the fundamental elements that secure business continuity by protecting organizations' information assets. Planned and secure backup structures must be established against potential data losses resulting from systemic failures, user errors, ransomware, physical damages, or cyber attacks.

Under the provided service, regular, automated, and versioned backup solutions are configured for file servers, client computers, email systems, virtual machines, and critical applications. Backups are performed locally (NAS, external drives) or on cloud-based platforms in an encrypted and access-controlled manner.

In backup infrastructures, backup frequency, version policies, retention periods, replication options, and rapid restore scenarios are planned specifically for the organization's needs. Furthermore, the success of backup jobs is regularly monitored, and retrospective reporting is provided.

Within the scope of disaster recovery scenarios, rapid restore plans from backups can be implemented in the event of a system crash or data corruption, ensuring minimal downtime.

Data backup service not only prevents information loss; it also creates a structural safeguard against risks as a robust component of the business continuity strategy.
