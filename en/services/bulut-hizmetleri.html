CLOUD SERVICES – MANAGEMENT OF CLOUD INFRASTRUCTURES AND EMAIL SYSTEMS

Cloud technologies enable the restructuring of corporate IT infrastructures in terms of flexibility, accessibility, and cost advantages. A well-planned and managed cloud infrastructure offers businesses high availability, secure data hosting, centralized management, and operational efficiency.

Hizmet kapsamında; Microsoft 365, Google Workspace and other corporate cloud solutions e-posta, takvim, belge paylaşımı ve iş birliği araçlarının yapılandırılması ve yönetimi sağlanmaktadır. E-posta sistemlerinde kullanıcı hesapları oluşturulmakta, lisans atamaları yapılmakta, güvenlik politikaları tanımlanmakta ve posta kutuları yapılandırılmaktadır.

Moreover; file storage and sharing platforms (OneDrive, Google Drive), collaborative workspaces (SharePoint, Google Sites), cloud-based security settings, authentication rules, and management console usage are centrally managed.

Storing internal correspondence, data, and documents in the cloud increases resilience against disaster scenarios, facilitates access, and ensures continuity in business processes. For security, email communication is protected with authentication protocols like SPF, DKIM, and DMARC, while advanced filtering systems are deployed for suspicious content.

Cloud services management is not merely a technical transition, but a holistic transformation process that enhances operational flexibility and adapts to modern work environments.
